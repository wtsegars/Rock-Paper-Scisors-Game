Volcano, Lizard, Spock

How to Play

This is like your typical game of rock, paper, scisors only instead of using rock, paper, scisors you will be using volcano, lizard, Spock. In this game, volcano beats Spock, Spock beats lizard, and lizard beats volcano. All you have to do is enter your name in the field below and wait for a second player to show up (it will notify you in the chatbox below). Once a player has arrived, then you can pick one of the three options mentioned above and your opponent will do the same. Scores are totaled as wins, loses, and ties and will be displayed on either side of the screen. The chatbox below can be used to communicate with your opponent. Good luck and have fun playing!
